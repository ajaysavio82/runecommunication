Welcome

Wendy Kohn, MS, CCC-SLP is a culture and communication specialist and speech-language pathologist based in Massachusetts. Her combined background in communication disorders and teaching English and professional communication skills to non-native speakers has given her a multi-faceted understanding of human communication from an intercultural as well as scientific perspective.


With work experience in India, Mexico, New York City, and her native Boston, she has coached and trained professionals from more than 30 countries. Her clients have come from such diverse work backgrounds as information technology, higher education, law, business consulting and finance. She spent two years at Microsoft’s largest technical support center in India offering customized coaching on communication and intercultural skills to engineers offering top-level support to customers around the globe.


Having lived abroad herself, she draws on her own experiences with the challenges of communicating across cultural and linguistic boundaries. She is most energized by finding people’s unique strengths and helping them express themselves authentically and with pride. Clients appreciate her compassionate, down-to-earth style and subject matter expertise. She offers flexible training programs, meeting the needs of those who appreciate the measurability and predictability of structure, as well as those seeking a more flexible and customized approach.
